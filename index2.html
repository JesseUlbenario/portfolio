What is FreshSense?

A start-up smell detecting systemthat helps farmers detect unnatural things in their crops.

How Does it Work?

Odor sensor

FreshSense is equipped with a smart odor sensor that detects a mushrooms from the air and alerts if there's early signs of trouble. Setting up only takes less than a minute without any need for sun and water.

Integrated Mobile App

The App shows what Freshsense is detecting. The color stays green whether theres nothing happening. When it changes, it alerts the farmer, some messages may include:

  • "Warning: Possible mold activity in Room 2"
  • "Alert: Air quality changing. Check humidity or ventilation."
